Changes

  • Added Firelands raid in Cataclysm.
  • Removed belt tokens from Blackwing Lair in Season of Discovery.
  • Added Hoodoo Pile items (hidden by default) to Zul'Gurub in Era and Season of Discovery.

Fixes

  • Fixed an issue where the distribute button would show the wrong text while trading an item in an instance during combat.
  • Players whose addons are out of date are now treated as though they don't have the addon installed, and will receive communications over whispers (if enabled).
  • The button to whisper rolls or reserves to your target now works for players who are not in the same group.
  • Primal Hakkari Idols in Season of Discovery now work the same way as in Era with regard to the Smart Reserves Removal option.
  • Players attempting to reserve Rune of Metamorphosis in Season of Discovery should now correctly be redirected to Shapeshifter's Sigil.
